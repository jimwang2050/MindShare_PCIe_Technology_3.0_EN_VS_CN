## **Prerequisite Knowledge** 

To get the full benefit of this material, it’s recommended that the reader have a reasonable background in PC architecture, including knowledge of an I/O bus and its related protocol. Because PCI Express maintains several levels of com‐ patibility with the original PCI design, critical background information regard‐ ing PCI has been incorporated into this book. However, the reader may well find it beneficial to read the MindShare book _PCI System Architecture_ .
